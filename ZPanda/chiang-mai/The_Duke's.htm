Food delivery from The Duke's @MAYA in Chiang Mai

The Duke's @MAYA is a popular American restaurant, located in the Maya Shopping Mall, in the heart of Chiang Mai, Thailand. The Duke's chain was founded in 2005, with a vision to provide great food for family and friends. Fresh beef plays a big part on their menu, and all steaks are imported directly from Australia, with other items sourced from Bangkok. All dishes are cooked to order with sauces, soups and cakes prepared daily.

The Duke's @MAYA popular dishes

With an emphasis on tasty American-style food, all dishes are available to order online for fast delivery, bringing classic burgers, pizza and pasta direct to your door. The special combination orders come with the added bonus of a free litre of lemonade or iced tea. The most popular dishes always include pizzas with your choice of fresh toppings, steaks aged to perfection and cooked to your preference and juicy burgers with American cheese.

  • ✓ Pizza: Available to order in three sizes, with fresh dough and a choice of over 20 toppings including genuine Italian sausage, pepperoni and ham.
  • ✓ Steak: Whether your choice is prime sirloin, boneless rib-eye or filet mignon, all the cuts are cooked to perfection and served with house fries and a sauce.
  • ✓ Burgers: Made of 100% imported beef, the burgers are served on toasted buns with a variety of options to enhance that American experience, including chilli, Cheddar and grilled onions.
