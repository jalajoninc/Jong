About Dean & Deluca in Bangkok

In 1977 Giorgio Deluca and Joel Dean opened their first store in Manhattan’s SoHo in America. The two men had a passion for food of high quality and taste, and have continued over the years to offer unique products sourced from around the world. Their brand stared to extend beyond the American shores in 2003 and now boast stores and restaurants in Tokyo, Bahrain, Seoul, Singapore, Dubai, Kuwait, Kuala Lumpur, Manila and Bangkok. The main outlet in Thailand is based in the upmarket Mahanakhon Cube lifestyle and retail centre and there are many smart Dean & Deluca cafes in key locations throughout the city of Bangkok as well. Visit foodpanda and place an online order for home delivery of some of the delicious food served at Dean & Deluca.

Dean & Deluca popular dishes

For a fantastic selection of beautifully presented, healthy, freshly prepared, tasty food browse through the tempting menus on foodpanda. You will find soups, salads, sandwiches, pizzas and wholesome main courses. You may not be able to resist the cake and bakery treats, such as the Rainbow Cake and Coffee Buttercream with Caramelised Almond cake. Their selection of beverage is extensive, from speciality teas and coffees, iced drinks, frappe, milkshakes, smoothies and New York sodas.

  • ✓ California Cobb: grilled chicken breast and dry cured bacon served with avocado, blue cheese, egg, mixed salad and a red wine vinaigrette.
