Hua Seng Hong, a menu of good, modern Chinese food

Love Chinese food? Then you'll love this restaurant. Well known and with an open minded approach to cuisine, Hua Seng Hong isn't your typical Chinese restaurant. Reacting to the wide cultural range of people who now live in Hong Kong, this is a place that has really embraced modern fusion cuisine styles. You'll find flavours of Japan like Wasabi, and ideas from Thai food favouring their dishes, which are imaginative and a good choice for casual entertaining and business dining too. So read on for more information about this friendly place to order online from for any occasion.

What's on the menu at Hua Seng Hong for delivery?

So, what's on offer and what occasion is this restaurant great for? This is a wonderful place for families, as lots of the food is healthy and typically Chinese. However, there's also a wide Dim Sum menu for office lunches which is a great way to impress clients, or makes for a great Friday night in treat with a bunch of friends.

  • ✓ 'Crisp Pig Ears with Shanghai Sauce' followed by 'Roasted Duck on Rice' makes a substantial, traditionally Chinese, Sunday lunch for family or friends.
  • ✓ Try the, 'Spicy Soup with Seafood' for a light, healthy lunch.
  • ✓ 'Steamed Sea Bass in Spicy Lime Sauce', with 'Stir Fried Watercress, Chilli and Oyster Sauce' would make a spectacular dinner with a hint of Thai influence, and would make a great celebration dinner for your loved one.
