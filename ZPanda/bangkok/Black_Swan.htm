The Black Swan menu for delivery in Bangkok

The world is full of different cuisines, and as people travel from different cultures, totally different ideas spread! This has led to a wide range of cuisines being available in Bangkok, and from even as far away as the United Kingdom! So if you want to try British food, or already like it, take a look at The Black Swan Menu. Based upon traditional recipes, served in a British Pub style atmosphere, and meat of a very high quality, the food here is very substantial, and the welcome is warm.

Delicious hearty food on the Black Swan menu

There's also lots of delicious relishes, mustards, gravies and sauces which make British food very flexible and a safe bet for business dining as well as entertaining people from different backgrounds. This is because, with sauces served on the side, everyone can have their dinner the way they like it. Britain is also famous for 'The English Breakfast,' which is probably one of the best breakfasts to enjoy if you've got up late, or are very hungry. Tempted? There's also some rich puddings on offer, and great snacks too, so read on for a few ideas.

  • ✓ Try a, 'Big Yorkie Classic.' With a choice of Pork/Lamb or Beef, roasted to perfection, and served with fresh vegetables and rich gravy on the side.
  • ✓ 'Bangers and Mash,' with special gravy, and vegetables is a comforting, tasty pork treat for any lunchtime. Perhaps try a sweet, rich 'Treacle sponge,' for dessert if you're really hungry.
  • ✓ 'The Full Monty,' is the biggest English Breakfast ever! Egg, Sausage, bacon, bubble & squeak, beans, tomatoes and mushrooms. This will set you up for the day for sure!
