Pala Pizza Romana delivery, a taste of Rome in Bangkok

Italian food is well known all over the world because of the popularity of Pizza. However, the rest of the Italian cuisine is also delicious and a wonderful style of food for special occasions and for everyday eating. There are also regional variations and Pala Pizza Romana celebrates the food of the middle of Italy and the south. This of course does mean Pizza too, because the south of Italy is where it is originally from but, what amazing pizzas this restaurant does! So, if you're looking for a Saturday night treat to go with a glass of wine and a great film at home, then look no further.

Pala Pizza Romana menu and lots more to order online

This restaurant really has a great menu, and has Italian items not usually seen so far from Italy, so the food has a really authentic feel to it. So next time you've had a busy week but want a top notch Pizza give them a try. They also have some good breakfast dishes too, and great Italian style coffees and desserts like Panna Cotta and Tiramisu, but see below for some more ideas. And next time you get up late on a Sunday, why not order breakfast from them and have truly relaxed end to the week.

  • ✓ Try a 'Toscana slice' for lunch, made with Tuscany Ham, Mozzarella and tomatoes.
  • ✓ 'Brie and Speck' is also a very European, tasty combination for a Pizza.
  • ✓ Perhaps order an 'Espresso Macchiato' and a 'Bacon Omelette' for a robust breakfast.
